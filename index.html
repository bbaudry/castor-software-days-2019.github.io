Castor Software Days is a three days event in Stockholm (Oct. 16-18, 2019) open to KTH students and researchers as well as external researchers from industry and other universities. The goal of this event is to present the software research activities at KTH, and provide reseachers opportunities and space for networking. The event consists of three tracks:

  • DevOps track: Test Automation, Fault Injection, Automatic Repair, Diversification, Randomization, Chaos Engineering
  • Security and Privacy track: Virtualisation, Compilers, Language Design, Formal Methods, Type System, Execution Platforms
  • Distributed Systems: Bigdata Analytics, Data-Intensive Platforms, Distributed Algorithms, Database Management Systems, Blockchain
